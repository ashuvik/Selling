Information about Himalaya Gasex Tablet

It is used to treat indigestion and its symptoms such as the feeling of fullness, dyspepsia, and nausea. It also helps to relieve symptoms such as belching, abdominal distension (bloating), and flatulence. It is also recommended for the preparation of abdominal X-rays before undergoing radiological examination. It can also be used to provide relief from abdominal discomfort post-surgery and also in case of prolonged immobilization. It exhibits carminative and antispasmodic properties which improve digestive function and treat indigestion and other gastric troubles.
It contains Ayurvedic herbs such as sunthi (dried ginger), triphala, vidange and other ingredients that play a key role in digestion. Ginger stimulates the secretion of gastric juices and helps in digestion. It exhibits prokinetic activity and helps normalise intestinal transit time and provides relief from postprandial distress. Triphala helps in digestion and intestinal motility along with providing relief from gaseous distension.
As this tablet is made from natural herbs, it is seen to be safe. However, if you are allergic to any of its ingredients, then do let your doctor know about it. Keep it out of reach of children and store it in a cool, dry place away from the sunlight. In case of any side effects or if your symptoms fail to improve, do consult your doctor at the earliest.

Directions to use:

Use it as directed by your doctor.
Ideally, it is advised to take 2 to 3 tablets after meals or when required.

Side effects of Himalaya Gasex Tablet:

These tablets are usually safe and don’t cause any side effects when taken as per a doctor's recommendation.
However, you must get in touch with your doctor if you experience any side effects or if your symptoms fail to improve even after taking the medicine as recommended by your doctor.
